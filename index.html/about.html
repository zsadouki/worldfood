About

El-Ghazali supplementary Arabic school teaches the Arabic language, Islamic studies and the Quran every Saturday from 9:00 to 14:00. Our teaching is overseen by a group of teachers academically distinguished and experienced in education. Teaching is open to all levels from reception to AS and A2 level and ages range from 5 to 16 years. We aim to revive the Arabic tongue and connect each member with his or her language and culture.

We believe all children have the right to enjoy learning in a fun and safe environment. All our staff and volunteers are carefully selected and fully vetted with enhanced DBS checks. We also arrange parents meetings, sports competitions and educational and enriching school trips.
