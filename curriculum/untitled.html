Curriculum

El-Ghazali Arabic School provides classes for boys and girls between the age of 5 and 16. The school uses several teaching techniques to follow the curriculum including dual language stories, picture books, videos, interactive ahite board and educational games and activities.

Lessons are carefully planned to meet the needs of the classes and to keep the students engaged. The curriculm adopted is the national curriculum used in Algeria, with levels ranging from reception to A level.

The school day consists of a whole school assembley every morning followed by two lessons a tea break then a lesson a lunch break and finally the last lesson. Throughout the day all classes have two hours of Arabic lessons, one hour of personal development and one hour of Quranic studies.
